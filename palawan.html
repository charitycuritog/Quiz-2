Palawan, one of the best places to visit in the Philippines,
is consistently ranked as one of the best islands in the world, and for a good reason.
It boasts white sand beaches and islands, clear blue waters,
a spectacular variety of marine life and shipwreck sites,
and majestic towering limestone cliffs.
